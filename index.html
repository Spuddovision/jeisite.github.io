WHAT IS STEEL SHADOWS?

Genre: Dark Sci-Fi, Rogue-Like, Platformer, Shooter.

“A first person horror shooter game set within a dark mechanical labyrinth, with the possibility of enemies, traps and treasure around every corner…”
The player wakes up to find themselves within an unfamiliar maze, with walls toweering into infinitum, one shudders to think how truly massive the structure really is. Fight, Loot and make death defying leaps of faith deeper and deeper into the labyrinth in a vain attempt to locate an exit.
